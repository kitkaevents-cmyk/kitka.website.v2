Services

Planning, Tailored to How Much You Want to Hold

From full end-to-end planning to a single, flawless day of coordination — choose the level of support that fits you.

For couples starting from scratch, planning remotely

Full Planning

9–12 months of planning, start to finish. We take full ownership of the day so you can experience it, not manage it.

  • Venue sourcing & booking
  • Full vendor team (florals, catering, music, photo/video)
  • Design, styling & budget management
  • Guest logistics coordination
  • On-site coordination (2–3 days)
€4,500–8,500 Flat fee, or 12–15% of total wedding budget
For couples who already have a venue booked

Partial Planning

4–6 months of focused planning to bring the rest of the day together around your venue.

  • Vendor sourcing & booking
  • Design & styling
  • Full-day timeline
  • On-site coordination (1–2 days)
€2,500–4,500 Flat fee
For couples who've planned everything themselves

Month-Of Coordination

We step in 4–6 weeks out to confirm every detail and run the day itself, flawlessly.

  • Final vendor confirmations
  • Timeline build & rehearsal
  • Full day-of coordination (1 day)
€1,200–2,000 Flat fee · +€300–500 per extra event day
For intimate ceremonies, under 20 guests

Elopement & Micro-Wedding

A smaller footprint, the same care — perfect for couples who want Bulgaria to themselves.

  • Venue & officiant
  • Core vendor team
  • On-site coordination (1 day)
€1,800–3,000 Flat fee

Guest Experience Add-On

Traveling with a big group? Add group travel coordination, a welcome event, and guided activity itineraries to any package — typically €800–2,000.

Prices reflect the current Bulgarian wedding market and are reviewed periodically.
